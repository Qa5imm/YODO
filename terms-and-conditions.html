General Guidlines

  • 1. To ensure the safety of our customers, we have made it mandatory for all riders to wear helmets during their rides. Helmets are placed in the box below the seat.
  • 2. Failure to report damages that occurred during the ride will result in the termination of services.
  • 3. You must have a valid Driving license to avail the bike rental service.
  • 4. Your rental period starts 5 minutes after the confirmation of the ride and ends when keys are returned to the YODO Management.

Violations and fines

Triple sawari

Failure to adhere to the two-person bike limit will result in a violation.

  • First violation: Rs. 1000 fine
  • Second violation : Rs. 2000 fine + Red listed
  • Third violation: Blacklist - suspension of services

Reckless driving

Sudden acceleration or deceleration without regard for surrounding traffic conditions, disregarding the safety of other people is a violation. Skidding, drifting, and all other such stuff also counts as reckless driving.

  • First violation : Warning
  • Second violation: Rs. 1000 fine
  • Third violation : Rs. 2000 fine + Red listed
  • Fourth violation: Blacklist - suspension of services

Overspeeding

Exceeding the speed limits defined by LUMS administration is a violation.

  • First violation : Warning
  • Second violation: Rs. 1000 fine
  • Third violation : Rs. 2000 fine + Red listed
  • Fourth violation: Blacklist - suspension of services

Driving in unauthorised zones

Driving in locations where access is restricted or prohibited under standard circumstances constitutes a violation.

  • First violation: Rs. 1000 fine
  • Second violation : Rs. 2000 fine + Red listed
  • Third violation: Blacklist - suspension of services
